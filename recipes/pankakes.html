My-Hop Pancakes

Desciprion

These delicious breakfast cakes are said to resemble those from a famous pancake house. When replacing milk for buttermilk, increase lemon juice to 1 1/2 tablespoons. Serve topped with butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • half cup vegetable oil
  • half teaspoon vanilla extract
  • half cup white sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teastpoon baking soda
  • 1 dash salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix buttermilk
  3. Whisk egg and lemon juice together in a separate bowl
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and grease with the butter
  5. Flip with spatula and brown the other side, about 2 more minutes
  6. Enjoy!
